# Data Retention Policy
## Per Use Case
  - Recruitment: 6 bulan (after hire decision)
  - Tenant: 1 tahun (after lease ends)
  - Customer: 2 tahun (after relationship ends)
  - Risk assessment: custom (per approval)

## Data Deletion
  - Auto-delete sesuai retention policy
  - Manual delete option (dengan approval)
  - Proof of deletion certificate
