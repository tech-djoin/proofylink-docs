# Compliance Checklist

## Requirement untuk enterprise
  - ✅ Personal Data Protection Act compliance (jika di Indonesia → GDPR, Data Privacy Law)
  - ✅ Bank Indonesia (BI) regulation
  - ✅ Financial Services Authority (OJK) regulation
  - ✅ Internal data governance policy
  - ✅ Staff training on data privacy
  - ✅ Breach notification procedure
  - ✅ Data processing agreement (DPA) dengan ProofyLink
