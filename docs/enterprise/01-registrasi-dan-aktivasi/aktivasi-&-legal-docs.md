# Aktivasi & Legal Docs

## Upload Dokumen Legal
  - Dokumen yang diperlukan (checklist):
    - Certificate of incorporation / SIUP / Izin usaha
    - NPWP & PPN number (jika applicable)
    - Authorized signatory list
    - Company letterhead sample
    - Bank account verification
  - Upload ke dashboard
  - Status: Waiting for review

## Review & Approval (Biro Kredit Side)
  - Timeline: 3-5 business days
  - Status tracking di dashboard
  - Email notification saat approve/reject

## Sign GTC (General Terms & Conditions)
  - Display GTC document di dashboard
  - Electronic signature (e-sign)
  - Timestamp & audit trail

## Sign STC (Service Terms & Conditions) per Use Case
  - Different STC per use case (Recruitment vs Tenant vs Customer)
  - Display relevant STC
  - E-sign confirmation
  - Status: Signed
